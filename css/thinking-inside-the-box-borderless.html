The Cottage Garden

Thecottage Garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design,dense pantings, and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can ba traced back for centuries,although they were re-invented in 1870's England,when stylized versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained Endlish estate gardens

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants,with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs,along with some fruit trees.
